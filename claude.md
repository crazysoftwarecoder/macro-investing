# Project Guidelines

## Excel Formatting

- All cell values should be center-aligned (horizontal and vertical)

## Release Schedules

### Job Market
- Unemployment Rate: Released first Friday of every month (with Nonfarm Payrolls)
- Initial Jobless Claims: Released weekly on Thursday
- Nonfarm Payrolls: Released first Friday of every month

### Inflation
- CPI YoY: Released monthly around 10th-15th
- Core CPI YoY: Released monthly around 10th-15th (with CPI)
- PPI MoM: Released monthly around 11th-14th

### Economic Activities
- ISM Manufacturing PMI: Released first business day of each month
- ISM Non-Manufacturing PMI: Released third business day of each month
- Chicago PMI: Released last business day of each month (requires MNI subscription - manual entry)
- Consumer Confidence: Released last Tuesday of each month

## Data Sources

### Official Sources
- **Unemployment Rate, Nonfarm Payrolls, CPI, Core CPI, PPI**: Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) - https://www.bls.gov
- **Initial Jobless Claims**: Department of Labor - https://www.dol.gov
- **ISM Manufacturing PMI, ISM Services PMI**: Institute for Supply Management (ISM) press releases via PR Newswire
- **Consumer Confidence**: The Conference Board - https://www.conference-board.org
- **Chicago PMI**: MNI Indicators (subscription required) - https://www.mnimarkets.com

### Notes
- Chicago PMI requires a paid subscription to MNI Indicators for official data access. The script will print instructions for manual entry.
